Danny Daniels was born on November 1, 1927 in Georgetown, British Guiana. He was an actor, known for Naked Gun 33 1/3: The Final Insult (1994), Ace Ventura: When Nature Calls (1995) and The Saint (1962). He was previously married to Berenice Grant. He died on December 4, 2010 in Inglewood, California, USA.
